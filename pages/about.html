About us

Code IT is one of the best inclusive computer training institutes in Purwanchal, Nepal. Established in 2017, our professional IT Training and Development center has been employing experts in this field to impart professional education to trainees.

Code IT is a software product development company. We are early technology adopters and rockstar innovators in providing the best quality services and make a team contributing to the development of world technologies. We build a community of developers and professionals interested in doing complex software projects.

We know how to transform business ideas into IT products that do work and help you grow in a dynamic market. By virtue of customer satisfaction and self-development, everyone gains personal benefits.
